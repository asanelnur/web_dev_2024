Travel to Kazakhstan!

Info:

Kazakhstan is the richest country in Central Asia, due to its large oil and natural gas reserves. The country inherited the culture of the ancient Silk Road, Nomadic lifestyle and the Soviet Union which had a great influence on its formation.

History of Kazakhstan:

Mongol tribes led by Genghis Khan invade Kazakhstan and Central Asia. Later they become assimilated by Turkic tribes that make up the majority in their empire. Late 15th century - With the formation of the Kazakh khanate, the Kazakhs emerge as a distinct ethnic group.

  • Kolsai and Kaindy Lakes
  • Paradise under heaven, Taban-Karagai plateau
